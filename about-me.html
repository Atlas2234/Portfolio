about me

who am I?

academics

I am a current student at University of Toronto Software Engineering co-op stream and I graduated from Thornhill Secondary School.

hobbies

My main hobbies include playing video games, making video games, listening to music, playing musical instruments, peaceful walks in nature, and watching youtube videos/movies.

Most of my skills stemmed from my hobbies. My fascination with music inspired me to start playing musical instruments. The languages and frameworks that I know include C, C#, Java, MIPS Assembly, SQLite, Javascript, Python, Python Flask, HTML, and CSS. I have also had an interest in art and thus have taken art lessons. I can make pencil sketches, pen sketches, acrylic paintings, and oil paintings; I also experimented with photoshop art and done some artwork on that as well. I recently also took an interest in pixel art and have been doing some small drawings with Piskel. You can find some of my artwork on youtube and the some more on my DeviantArt account.
